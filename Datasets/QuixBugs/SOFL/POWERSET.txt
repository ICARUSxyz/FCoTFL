Pre/Post Specification：
module POWERSET
type 
    arr: sequence of any
    pset: sequence of sequence of any

process powerset(arr: sequence of any) pset: sequence of sequence of any
pre  
    true
post 
    ∀ subset ∈ pset • subset ⊆ arr ∧
    ∀ s ⊆ arr • s ∈ pset ∧
    length(pset) = 2^length(arr)
end_process

end_module
